New! Quests​
​
Get ready for a big adventure with our new update. ​
We're bringing in something cool - the all-new Quest System.​
​
No more Player Levels.​
Now, instead of just leveling up, players get to do quests.​
This makes our game more exciting and fun, and you decide when you want to get ready for a quest to earn great rewards, rather than following the same line of levels every season.​
​
What's a quest?​
You can buy quests at the Quest Shop! The number of quests you can buy might change based on your rank. After you buy quests from the Quests NPC, you have a certain time to finish them. These quests are challenging and fun. Plus, you can see all the tasks and rewards you might get. But, you'll get one randomly chosen for you based on what kind of quest you buy.​
​
How do you buy quests?​
As we said, you get them at the Quest Shop. There are 5 kinds: Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, and Mythical. The more special the quest, the more it costs, the harder it is, but the rewards are better too!​



New! Events​

We're bringing you something really special - Events, happening five times every week! ​
These aren't just regular game activities. They're big events that are all about bringing our gaming community together for some awesome times.​
​
Every week, you get five chances to join in on these cool events. They're specially made to be fun, challenging, and full of rewards. No matter if you like playing alone or with a team, there's something fun for everyone.​
​
You can join these events in their special world. Just go through the Event Portal in the PvP arena. This portal opens for three minutes when an event starts, but after that, you can't join the ongoing event.​
​
Be ready for a real challenge, especially in the events with PvP. ​
These are intense because you can't leave until the end, and almost all commands are turned off. ​
Winning one of these events is a big deal and exciting.​
​
And guess what? We've added 4 brand-new events for this season. They are:​
Boss Battle​
Envoys​
Dungeons​
Stronghold​
In this epic showdown, you'll face off against an incredibly powerful boss.

Only the top three players who deal the most damage and remain standing until the end will be crowned the winners of the Boss Battle.		You'll spawn into a vast room brimming with supply crates, each filled with exciting loot.
Your mission? Gather as much treasure as you can in 15 minutes. But here's the twist - there's no single winner in this challenge.

When the 15 minutes are up, a portal will open, offering you a one-way ticket out of the room with all your gathered goods.

PvP is enabled within this event.		In the Dungeons, you'll encounter mobs with formidable health. As you navigate the labyrinthine passages, keep an eye out for a special drop place.

Hidden in a random location within the dungeon, this spot holds a coveted item, different every single time.

PvP is enabled within this event.		The Stronghold is essentially KOTH but on a larger scale.
You and your gang can enter the Stronghold and capture it for a great reward.

Once you have entered the Stronghold, the only way out is hiding, dying, or winning the Stronghold.

PvP is enabled within this event.



New! Fruit Harvester Hoe​

Welcome the Fruit Harvester Hoe, the latest cool tool for your game. It's here to change the way you play. ​
This hoe is special â€“ it's made to take the place of the pickaxe enchantments for fruit.​
​
How to get the Fruit Harvester Hoe?​
Getting this amazing hoe is a bit about luck. Keep an eye on the token shop because the Fruit Harvester Hoe will show up there sometimes. ​
When it does, you have the chance to get it.​
​
Upgrade your Fruit Harvester Hoe!​
To make your Fruit Harvester Hoe better, you'll need enchantment books. ​
These books are important to improve what the hoe can do. You can buy these books and use them to add different enchantment levels to your hoe.​




New! Pickaxe Abilities​
​
We've got something cool for you within the next season - new Pickaxe Abilities! ​
They're here to make your mining adventure even more worth your time.​
​
A set of boosts for each pickaxe​
Now, every pickaxe you find in loot crates, win from our new events, or buy from the token shop comes with a special power. ​
These powers give you a big boost to help you get more money, tokens, experience, and much more!​
​
Every pickaxe is different, each with its unique ability. This means you can collect different pickaxes with different powers, which will help you a lot in your search for treasures in the game.​
​
The following 5 Pickaxe Abilities are available for this season:​
Money Boost Ability​
Token Boost Ability​
EXP Boost Ability​
Shiny Diamonds Boost Ability​
Beacons Boost Ability​

​

Updated! Pickaxe Enchantments​

We've made some really important changes to the pickaxe enchantments in our game. Our aim was to make things simpler and fairer for all players.​
​
First off, we've taken away some enchantments. This makes it easier for you to understand and choose the right ones for how you play. Now, it's going to be much clearer and easier to pick an enchantment.​
​
And there's more - we've also adjusted the prices for the enchantments we kept. ​
We want to make sure the prices are fair and affordable for everyone, and that they make sense for how useful each enchantment is in the game.​
​
The following Pickaxe Enchants will be available this upcoming season:​
Efficiency FortuneVeinminerJackhammer
ExplosiveLaserTreasure SeekerExperience
Beacon FinderToken GreedCharityPurity
Loot Box BoosterPrestige FinderBoomerang


Updated! Fruit​
​
We know you put in a lot of effort and time to harvest fruits, so we're adding something extra to make it more fun.​
​
Now, get experience when you harvest!​
From now on, every time you mine a fruit, you'll get some experience points. This means when you use your hoe to collect fruits, you're not just getting the fruits - you're also moving forward in the game.​
​
Updated prestige requirements​
We've been using the old prestige system for fruit harvesting for a while. This season, we're changing it up. We've adjusted which fruits you can harvest at each prestige level to match our updated system.​


Updated! Crates​

We have done a major overhaul to the crates, making them worth their rarity. We noticed the economy is too focused on keys, and it rightfully was. ​
The rewards in some keys were better than a key of a higher rarity, and for this season we have tackled this issue and have taken the time to completely rebalance out the crates for what they are supposed to be.​
​
Every crate has been rebalanced​
All the rewards inside have been updated, so get ready for a whole new level of excitement when you open them!​
Inside, you'll find lots of new and rare items that are cool, and we've kept all the popular items that our players love.​



Updated! EXP Shop​

We've made some updates to the EXP Shop to make the game more challenging.​
​
Now, when you buy enchantment books from the EXP Shop, the level of enchantment you get will be random. ​
It won't always be the highest level anymore. ​
This means it's a bit more of a challenge to build your perfect set of tools and gear, but it's also more interesting.​
​
With this new setup, players who spend time and work hard to gain experience will have a better chance of getting higher-level enchantments.​



Fixed! Bugs​
​
We did our best to polish and fix as many existing bugs inside the OpPrison server. We have looked through all the bug reports and managed to fix them. If you come across any bugs with the new release, make sure to report them at our forums under the bug reports section.​
Here are a few of the bugs we squished out:​
  • Fixed vouchers from breaking sometimes.
  • Fixed soul cave mobs leaving the soul cave.
  • Fixed a typo of compacted fruit still saying drugs.
  • Fixed experience booster not working.
  • Fixed beacon booster not working.
  • Fixed beacon finder enchantment.
  • Fixed upgrading backpacks from /tshop menu not working.
  • Fixed jackhammer showing incorrect beacon summary.
  • Fixed bombs leaving floating blocks behind.
​
​
Misc. Updates & Changes​
​
Multiple minor changes have been made, some of these small changes are the following:​
  • Removed boosters from the gold shop.
  • Added a broadcast for when a coinflip game has ended.
  • Removed coinflip taxes.
  • Made linking your Discord account a requirement to place inmates.
  • Nerfed the resource pet.
  • Added a new seasonal crate.
  • Added a new seasonal loot crate.
  • Added a message for when you can claim a new milestone.
  • Updated the milestone rewards.
Please note: Any suggestions for changes feel free to leave them in the comments.​


​
Question and Answers​
​
Why did OpPrison reset?​

OpPrison is already up for some time. A reset has to happen to change and improve with lots of excellent new features, to make the game more exciting for you all! This then allows you, the players, to have a fresh start and new players can begin to play without any significant disadvantage.​
​
This means all cells, items, money and the map will be reset. Nothing will be kept leftover from the previous map. I know this must be a disappointing revelation for a lot of you, but think about the exciting new changes, the new alliances you can forge, the new map, the new features and updates the different kinds of gameplay, and style is provided with this update will surely make it a worth-while and exciting reset!​
​
Below you can find what you will lose and what you will keep​
​
What will I keep?​
  • Purchased Ranks & Rank Upgrades
  • Purchased Perks
  • Won out crates commands/ranks

What will I lose?​
  • Your cells
  • All items in your inventory and ender chest
  • Any currencies such as money and exp
  • Chatcolor permissions.
  • Seasonal ranks and perks
  • Player levels & their colors + The premium pass




We hope to see you on our new OpPrison server soon!
If you have feedback or any suggestion, let us know we'd love to make this as good as we can.
Thanks

​
Get ready for a big adventure with our new update. ​
We're bringing in something cool - the all-new Quest System.​
​
No more Player Levels.​
Now, instead of just leveling up, players get to do quests.​
This makes our game more exciting and fun, and you decide when you want to get ready for a quest to earn great rewards, rather than following the same line of levels every season.​
​
What's a quest?​
You can buy quests at the Quest Shop! The number of quests you can buy might change based on your rank. After you buy quests from the Quests NPC, you have a certain time to finish them. These quests are challenging and fun. Plus, you can see all the tasks and rewards you might get. But, you'll get one randomly chosen for you based on what kind of quest you buy.​
​
How do you buy quests?​
As we said, you get them at the Quest Shop. There are 5 kinds: Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, and Mythical. The more special the quest, the more it costs, the harder it is, but the rewards are better too!​

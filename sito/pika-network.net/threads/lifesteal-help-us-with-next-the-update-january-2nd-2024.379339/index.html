Hello PikaNetwork!
​
A new year, a new chapter for the PikaNetwork Lifesteal, but we need your help! We wish to cooperate with the community on what they want to see for an upcoming reset of Lifesteal! As always, we are doing our best to keep you happy. It is very important that you, the player who plays on our network, share your public opinion. Within this post, the community can leave us suggestions and all your ideas for Lifesteal to make the best decisions together.​
​
If you're interested in helping with suggestions for changes and additions, you can use this thread to discuss anything with other people in our community that you think would be beneficial. Please also leave any bug reports, small changes, or anything at all (related to Lifesteal) on the post, but please be descriptive when giving feedback, and use details on what needs changes. Referring your suggestions/bug reports link in the thread reply is appreciated as well. We also did our best to scour every suggestions and bugs before asking everyone here, but if you think we have made an oversight, let us know.​

What major stuffs do you want to see?​

To start things off we want to ask you the question. What major changes or features would you like to see on the next Lifesteal reset? There are no limits on what you can suggest! To get that creative thinking going here is a list of some major updates and features that we have in mind.​
  • Pets Rework and Balance Changes! – With that going to happen in just around the corner, the pets in Lifesteal will get their rework and balance changes for the next season. And of course, there will be some new fluffy pets joining us very soon.
  • New Fishing System and Zone! – New adventures coming from a fishy place. Get ready to reel in the fun with all new fishes, enchants, rods, and more.
  • Skills Rework and Balancing Changes! – The skillful warriors of Lifesteal is getting more recognition and more wonderous perks.
  • Team Quests, Rewards, and Prestiges Overhaul! – Teamwork makes the dream work, and with this change, you'd never wanna go solo in the battlefield again! We are fixing the quests, and the faster you complete your team quests, the better rewards you'll receive as a team!

What Quality of Life changes do you want to see?​

Another critical question is... What existing feature should be improved for the next season? The following features are something that we have in mind to update and/or give a quality of life update.​
  • Potions and Enchantments Rework! – A magical change is coming to your brewed potions and heavy books of enchantment to balance out the fun in your combat.
  • Reworking Player Levels! – Seems tedious to complete? Fear not! Requirements for each levels will be decreased for a price of 150 more fresh levels with new rewards in rotation!
  • Server Crates Rework! – New bigger rewards along its requirements are being prepared for your next battle!
  • End Crystal Balancing Changes! – As much as we like to fight with our crystals, we have made following changes to the item from its pricing to making the item un-craftable by players!
  • Crafting Hearts! – Rejuvenate your precious hearts by crafting them with your materials and bare hands.
With all that being said it is now your turn to leave your suggestions underneath this post.​
Please note: Stick to the topic! Any off-topic/non-related to this game mode comments will be deleted.​

Please take this opportunity to let us know by replying to this message what changes you would like to see going forwards!
You can also let us know what you think of the current ideas we have and why we should add them or not. Thank you!​

Hiya Developers, In the upcoming season of lifesteal, I would highly recommend removing elytra from functioning in the spawn PvP zone, similar to how it has been implemented in /warp pvp. Having elytra enabled effectively removes the entire point of the combat tag system - to stop people escaping fights with the click of two buttons. Elytra enabled promotes an incredibly negative gameplay style, with it being difficult to gain kills, or capitalise off of opponents mistakes. As I said, this has already been implemented in /warp pvp, which resulted in a larger amount of people using it, due to the fairness it presented.

However. If you do remove Elytra from spawn PvP area, (In my opinion a necessity) please take into consideration the effect this will have on the number of players willing to venture into spawn pvp, and consequently the amount of PvP happening. I propose you increase the value of loot received from the regular drops event, to something that will be of value, regardless of how much the player has been playing. Currently, all the loot they give is useless to someone with more than 5 minutes playtime. If however, it were changed to instead give items of a rarer standing some of the time, like say a %5 chance for the rubbish armour to drop as netherite rather than diamond, it would greatly incentivize people to venture into spawn pvp, regardless of how easy it is to run from fights. Or even better, bring back the season 1 + 2 system, where there was a very small chance to get keys from the drops events. This should be implemented to KOTH as well, as the current koth rewards consit of oak logs and iron armour. Overall, this change combined with removing elytra would greatly improve player enjoyment, and stop the gamemode from failing to retain players.

Factions Revamp - Beta Season - PikaNetwork 2023
November 25th, 2023, 19:00 GMT / 13:00 EST / 12:00 CST / 10:00 PST / 00:30 IST
​
Hello, dear PikaNetwork players.

We're excited to announce the upcoming reset of the latest Factions season! You read that right, Factions, not OpFactions, the good old beloved Factions realm!

Embracing innovation, we've taken a bold step to try something new, and we can't wait to share it with our community.
This update is not just a regular reset; it's a transformation, bringing Factions into the modern era while staying true to our roots.

So, mark your calendars and get ready for a groundbreaking season of Factions!
We're upgrading to the latest Minecraft version, 1.20.2, ensuring a seamless blend of new features and classic 1.8 gameplay, but on the latest versions.

Prepare to immerse yourself in a world where tradition meets innovation. We're eager to see what you think of this exciting new chapter in Factions!

Are you ready to hop into a new season?! Say no more. The update will be released on:

November 25th, 2023
19:00 GMT / 13:00 EST / 12:00 CST / 10:00 PST / 00:30 IST
play.pika-network.net

Please note: The release time can be a couple of minutes later, we are trying to reach 7 PM CET.

Grace Period (no explosions): 7 days.
First hour: No flight, No wilderness command, No hunger loss, No ender pearls.
World Corners: 16
Faction shield: 18 hours
Cannoning speed: 3 seconds
Season length: 5 weeks

Changelog​
​
This revamp is a big one, so a lot of things have been removed, changed, and updated, buckle up and get ready to dive into the changelog for what the future of Factions has to offer at PikaNetwork.​
​
​
Latest version! While being faithful to 1.8​

In our newest Factions season, we're excited to run the game on Minecraft 1.20.2. But don't worry, we've worked hard to keep your favorite parts of the game the same. ​
​
Cannoning is close to 1.8 cannon mechanics.​
We've obtained a Faction jar that lets us almost perfectly copy the 1.8 version cannons. ​
These cannons are a bit different to work with the higher world, but switching to this new mode should feel smooth and familiar.​
​
We will be having 1.8 PvP​
When it comes to PvP, we know it's important to keep things the way our players like them. Even though we're updating to the latest Minecraft version, we want to make sure PvP feels like the good old 1.8 style. So, there won't be any crystals, elytras, or strong shields. We will have shields, but they'll be used more for blocking hits, like in the older version of Minecraft, and not as powerful as in the newest Minecraft version. We're doing this to make sure PvP stays fun and familiar for everyone.​
​
We know the update to the latest version might be something a lot of players do not like, but here at PikaNetwork we think these newer versions are the future of the game, we can not keep maintaining a server version that is reaching its decade-old birthday soon. The community is important to us, so by having a new factions on 1.20, while being faithful to 1.8 we think there is a best of both worlds. The server can be played from any version starting from 1.18, all the way up to the latest version (currently 1.20).​

We hope that you are also ready to move on and take a step into the future of the gamemodes, we have done our best to make something truly special with it.​

Express yourself​

Want to go back to the basics?​

Our game mode has been structured to accommodate gameplay without the resource pack. However, we enthusiastically recommend embracing the resource pack for a heightened experience. Remember, this resource pack is entirely optional, but we highly recommend trying it out to see how it transforms your Factions experience into something even more exciting and engaging. Dive into this enhanced world and discover all the new features waiting to make your game better than ever!​

Nether Wart Harvester Hoe! Your main source of income to start​

Every player will begin their journey with a special tool: the Netherwart Harvester Hoe. ​
This unique hoe is your key to starting your grind and earning money in the game.​
​
The Netherwart Harvester Hoe is designed specifically for harvesting fully grown nether warts. When you use it to break a fully matured nether wart, the hoe does two things automatically: it sells the nether wart for you and replants a new one in its place. This makes your farming process efficient and effortless, allowing you to focus on building up your resources and growing your faction's wealth and power.​
​

Souls! A currency to grind for​

We're excited to introduce a new in-game currency in our latest Factions season: Souls. ​
This ethereal currency is earned through the thrill of combat, both against other players and mobs within the game. ​
Every time you defeat a player or a mob, there is a chance you'll be rewarded with Souls.​
​
Spend your souls on the black market​
These hard-earned Souls can be spent in a special place: the Black Market. ​
The Black Market is an exclusive in-game shop where you can exchange Souls for rare and powerful items.​
​
The Black Market has a static shop, which has the same items every day, but also with a limited stock. ​
These items are certain armor pieces, creeper eggs, sell wands, sand wands, and much more.​
Next to the static Black Market shop there also is a rotating Black Market Shop, which has new and fresh limited items every day.​

Quests! Complete Quests and earn great rewards​
​
It's time to dive into an exhilarating update that will ignite your inner explorer. ​
Get ready to embark on a quest like never before, as we introduce the all-new Quest System.​
​
What are quests?​
You have the opportunity to purchase quests from the Quest Shop! The number of quests available may vary depending on your rank.​
Once you've acquired your quests that can be bought from the Quests NPC, you'll have a limited window of time to prove yourself by completing challenging tasks. ​
But that's not all! You can view all potential tasks and rewards, though you'll be assigned one at random based on the rarity that you purchase. ​
​
Once activated you have a certain time to complete your randomly assigned quest. ​
These quests are designed to give you a great boost, as you are limited to how many quests you can do per day.​
​
How to purchase quests?​
As mentioned before, you can purchase these from the Quest Shop, there are 5 different tiers, Common, Rare, Epic, Legendary, and Mythical. ​
The higher the tier, the bigger the costs, the harder the quest, but the better the rewards!​

Question and Answers​
​
Why did Factions reset?​

Factions is already up for some time. A reset has to happen to change and improve with new features, to make the game more exciting for you all! This then allows players to have a fresh start and new players can begin to play without any significant disadvantage.​

This means all items, money, and the map will be reset. Nothing will be kept leftover from the previous map. I know this must be a disappointing revelation for a lot of you, but think about the exciting new changes, the new alliances you can forge, the new map, the new features and updates the different kinds of gameplay, and the style provided with this update will surely make it a worth-while and exciting reset!​

Below you can find what you will lose and what you will keep.​
​
What will I keep?​
  • Purchased Ranks & Rank Upgrades
  • Purchased Perks
  • Won out crates ranks

What will I lose?​
  • Your claims
  • All items in your inventory and ender chest
  • Any currencies such as money and exp
  • Chatcolor permissions.
  • Seasonal ranks and perks

Latest version! While being faithful to 1.8​

In our newest Factions season, we're excited to run the game on Minecraft 1.20.2. But don't worry, we've worked hard to keep your favorite parts of the game the same. ​
​
Cannoning is close to 1.8 cannon mechanics.​
We've obtained a Faction jar that lets us almost perfectly copy the 1.8 version cannons. ​
These cannons are a bit different to work with the higher world, but switching to this new mode should feel smooth and familiar.​
​
We will be having 1.8 PvP​
When it comes to PvP, we know it's important to keep things the way our players like them. Even though we're updating to the latest Minecraft version, we want to make sure PvP feels like the good old 1.8 style. So, there won't be any crystals, elytras, or strong shields. We will have shields, but they'll be used more for blocking hits, like in the older version of Minecraft, and not as powerful as in the newest Minecraft version. We're doing this to make sure PvP stays fun and familiar for everyone.​
​
We know the update to the latest version might be something a lot of players do not like, but here at PikaNetwork we think these newer versions are the future of the game, we can not keep maintaining a server version that is reaching its decade-old birthday soon. The community is important to us, so by having a new factions on 1.20, while being faithful to 1.8 we think there is a best of both worlds. The server can be played from any version starting from 1.18, all the way up to the latest version (currently 1.20).​

We hope that you are also ready to move on and take a step into the future of the gamemodes, we have done our best to make something truly special with it.​

Why did Factions reset?​

Factions is already up for some time. A reset has to happen to change and improve with new features, to make the game more exciting for you all! This then allows players to have a fresh start and new players can begin to play without any significant disadvantage.​

This means all items, money, and the map will be reset. Nothing will be kept leftover from the previous map. What will I keep?​
  • Purchased Ranks & Rank Upgrades
  • Purchased Perks
  • Won out crates ranks

What will I lose?​

  • All items in your inventory and ender chest

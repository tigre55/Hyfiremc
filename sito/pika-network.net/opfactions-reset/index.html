Hello, dear PikaNetwork players.

Exciting update: The latest season of OpFactions is almost here!
We've been busy making loads of improvements and fixes to enhance your OpFactions experience.

Your feedback has been invaluable, and we've focused on refining the gameplay for a smoother experience.
Prepare yourself for an all-new season of OpFactions

And don't forget to explore the completely new darkzone and the fresh events we've added!

December 15th, 2023
18:00 GMT / 13:00 EST / 12:00 CST / 10:00 PST / 23:30 IST
play.pika-network.net

First hour: No flight, No wilderness command, No hunger loss, No ender pearls.

New! Events​

We're bringing you something really special - Events, happening five times every week! ​
These aren't just regular game activities. They're big events that are all about bringing our gaming community together for some awesome times.​
​
Every week, you get five chances to join in on these cool events. They're specially made to be fun, challenging, and full of rewards. No matter if you like playing alone or with a team, there's something fun for everyone.​

How to enter?​
You can join these events in their special world. Just go through the Event Portal in the PvP arena. ​
This portal opens for three minutes when an event starts, but after that, you can't join the ongoing event.​

...a high risk for a big reward.​
Be ready for a real challenge, especially in the events with PvP. ​
These are intense because you can't leave until the end, and almost all commands are turned off. ​
​
Winning one of these events is a big deal and exciting.​
And guess what? We've added 4 events for this season. They are:​
Boss Battle​
Envoys​
Dungeons​
Stronghold​
In this epic showdown, you'll face off against an incredibly powerful boss.

Only the top three players who deal the most damage and remain standing until the end will be crowned the winners of the Boss Battle.		You'll spawn into a vast room brimming with supply crates, each filled with exciting loot.
Your mission? Gather as much treasure as you can in 15 minutes. But here's the twist - there's no single winner in this challenge.

When the 15 minutes are up, a portal will open, offering you a one-way ticket out of the room with all your gathered goods.

Question and Answers​
​
Why did OpFactions reset?​

OpFactions is already up for some time. A reset has to happen to change and improve with new features, to make the game more exciting for you all! This then allows players to have a fresh start and new players can begin to play without any significant disadvantage.​

This means all items, money, and the map will be reset. Nothing will be kept leftover from the previous map. I know this must be a disappointing revelation for a lot of you, but think about the exciting new changes, the new alliances you can forge, the new map, the new features and updates the different kinds of gameplay, and the style provided with this update will surely make it a worth-while and exciting reset!​

Below you can find what you will lose and what you will keep.​
​
What will I keep?​
  • Purchased Ranks & Rank Upgrades
  • Purchased Perks
  • Won out crates commands/ranks

What will I lose?​
  • Your claims
  • Player level & premium reward pass
  • All items in your inventory and ender chest
  • Any currencies such as money and exp
  • Chatcolor permissions.
  • Seasonal ranks and perks




We hope to see you on our new OpFaction server soon!
If you have feedback or any suggestion, let us know we'd love to make this as good as we can.

Question and Answers​
​
Why did OpFactions reset?​

OpFactions is already up for some time. A reset has to happen to change and improve with new features, to make the game more exciting for you all! This then allows players to have a fresh start and new players can begin to play without any significant disadvantage.​

This means all items, money, and the map will be reset. Nothing will be kept leftover from the previous map. I know this must be a disappointing revelation for a lot of you, but think about the exciting new changes, the new alliances you can forge, the new map, the new features and updates the different kinds of gameplay, and the style provided with this update will surely make it a worth-while and exciting reset!​

Below you can find what you will lose and what you will keep.​
​
What will I keep?​
  • Purchased Ranks & Rank Upgrades
  • Purchased Perks
  • Won out crates commands/ranks

What will I lose?​
  • Your claims
  • Player level & premium reward pass
  • All items in your inventory and ender chest
  • Any currencies such as money and exp
  • Chatcolor permissions.
  • Seasonal ranks and perks
